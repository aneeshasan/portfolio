My Projects

PLC Automation for Steel Manufacturing

Objective: To automate the material handling processes in the steel manufacturing industry to improve efficiency, reduce manual intervention, and ensure consistent production quality.

Outcomes: Developed a comprehensive PLC program integrated with HMI and SCADA systems to control the operation of conveyors, cranes, and other automated machinery.

Results:

  • Increased production efficiency by 20% by streamlining operations.
  • Reduced operational errors, leading to a significant drop in material wastage.
  • Enhanced safety protocols with automated emergency stop features, minimizing workplace accidents.

  • Technologies Used: PLC, HMI, SCADA

Induction Motor Fault Detection System

Objective: To develop a robust fault detection system for induction motors to enhance predictive maintenance practices in manufacturing environments.

Outcomes: Leveraged signal processing techniques, including Wavelet Packet Transform (WPT) and Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), to analyze motor vibrations and detect anomalies.

Results:

  • Achieved a fault detection accuracy of 95%, allowing for timely maintenance interventions.
  • Reduced downtime by 30% through early identification of potential motor failures.
  • Decreased maintenance costs by 25% due to optimized maintenance schedules and reduced emergency repairs.

  • Technologies Used: MATLAB, WPT, FFT, Machine Learning
